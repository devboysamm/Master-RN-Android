Email support

How can we help?

Whether you have hit a bug, have a question about a lesson, or want to share feedback, send us an email at support@masterreactnative.me and we will get back to you. We typically reply within 2 business days.

To help us resolve things quickly, it is useful to include:

  • What you were trying to do and what happened instead.
  • The device and operating system version you are using.
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual.

Report a problem from the app

You can also reach us without leaving the app. Open the Profile tab, tap Report a bug, choose a category, and describe what went wrong. Reports come straight to our team.

Manage or delete your account

To delete your account and all of your data, open the Profile tab and tap Delete account. This is permanent and cannot be undone. If you would prefer we do it for you, email support@masterreactnative.me from the address on your account.
